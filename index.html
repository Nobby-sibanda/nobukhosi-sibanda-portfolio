About Me

Bridging Finance,
Data & Mathematics

NS

I'm Nobukhosi Sibanda, a Quantitative Analyst student with a strong foundation in both Data Science and Finance. I hold a Master's degree in Data Science (graduated May 2026) and a Bachelor's degree in Finance, giving me a rare dual lens through which I approach quantitative problems.

My work sits at the intersection of statistical modelling, machine learning, and financial theory — from building anomaly-detection pipelines and NLP trading signals to constructing eigenportfolios using Random Matrix Theory and developing volatility-scaled momentum strategies.

I'm passionate about turning complex, messy data into rigorous, actionable insight — whether that's pricing derivatives, forecasting store sales, or detecting fraud in credit card transactions.
